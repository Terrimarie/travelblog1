About Me

Jumping around the planet since 2009

Hey travellers! First time travellers, experienced travellers, nomads and everyone inbetween. I am Terri I have been travelling all my life and since 2009 I decided to become a nomad and travel the world permenantly. Let me tell you more about my story.

I have always had a passion for travelling. There is nothing I enjoy more that to explore new places, experincing new cultures and cuisines.

I've found that some of the best ways to find hidden gems is to get tips from fellow travellers. So I decided to share my experinces and tips using this blog. I hope you find it useful. If you have any questions, or tips you would like to share or any queries feel free to contact me via the contact page.
